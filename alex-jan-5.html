Math Worksheet Solutions

Problems 1–5
1.
Convert the improper fraction 20/6 to a mixed number and reduce.
• 20 ÷ 6 = 3 with a remainder of 2 → 3 2/6
• Reduce 2/6 to 1/3.
Answer: 3 1/3
2.
A fathom is 6 feet. How deep is 2 1/2 fathoms?
• 2.5 fathoms × 6 feet/fathom
• 2 × 6 = 12 feet
• 1/2 × 6 = 3 feet
• 12 + 3 = 15 feet
Answer: 15 feet
3.
Average cars per hour (2769 cars in 3 hours).
• 2769 ÷ 3 = 923
Answer: 923 cars
4.
5 1/2 - 1 1/3
• Common denominator is 6.
• 5 3/6 - 1 2/6 = 4 1/6
Answer: 4 1/6
5.
5 1/3 - 2 1/2
• Common denominator is 6: 5 2/6 - 2 3/6
• Borrow from 5: 4 8/6 - 2 3/6
• 4 - 2 = 2 and 8/6 - 3/6 = 5/6
Answer: 2 5/6
