Basic Rules

  • Beat dealer without going over 21
  • Face cards = 10, Aces = 1 or 11
  • Dealer must hit until 17
  • Blackjack (A + 10-card) beats everything

When You Should Hit

  • 12–16 when dealer shows 7–A
  • 11 or less: ALWAYS hit

When You Should Stand

  • 17 or higher
  • 12–16 when dealer shows 2–6

When You Should Double

  • 10 vs dealer 2–9
  • 11 vs dealer anything except Ace
  • Ace+6 vs dealer 3–6
