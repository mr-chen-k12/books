MTEL Disparities by Race

Comparing Passing Rates: Literacy vs. Math

The "Math Gap": While all groups see a drop in pass rates for Math, the impact is disproportionate. For Black/African American candidates, the passing rate for the Elementary Math requirement drops to roughly 27%.

Literacy (2024) vs. General Math (2022)

Detailed Breakdown

*Note: 2024 specific racial breakdown for Math was not fully extracted; 2022 General Curriculum Math data is used here as a representative proxy for ongoing structural trends.
